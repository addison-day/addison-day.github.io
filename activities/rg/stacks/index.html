Reading Group on Stacks

Fall 2022

This is a reading group convened to learn and understand the theory of stacks by reading Notes on Grothendieck topologies, fibered categories and descent theory by Angelo Vistoli.

Participants: Deewang Bhamidipati (Convener), Xu Gao, Shane Kennerly, David Rubinstein, Albert Zhang, Wade Hampton, Seiji Hansen, Tsz Yau Tin, Sam Miller
