UCSC Graduate Colloquium Companion Page

by Deewang Bhamidipati and David Rubinstein

This is a companion page for the UCSC Graduate Colloquium that is currently being organized by David and Deewang. Notes and other relevant resources will be stored on this page. You can find the current schedule (Fall 2021) of the colloquium here. The colloquium has been organized remotely since Fall 2020.
