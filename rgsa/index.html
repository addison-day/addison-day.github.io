Reading Group on Serre's A Course in Arithmetic

This was a reading group convened to read Serre's A Course in Arithmetic. Our primary goal was to read and understand the first part of the book: Algebraic Methods. To that end we organized weekly seminars where the regular participants presented sections from the book.

Regular Participants:  Deewang Bhamidipati (Convener), Malachi Alexander, Sandra Nair
